Hello, \n \n My name is Gilad Moskowitz and I am a mathematician, programmer, and avid fan of tabletop games. I completed my undergraduate education at UCLA where I double majored in Chemistry and Mathematics. I then took a small break from education to work and travel the world. I returned to school to pursue a M.S. in Applied Mathematics with a Concentration in Communication Systems at SDSU. My primary mathematic interests are number theory, abstract algebra, and combinatorics. I also enjoy applying machine learning and other algorithmic approaches to attempt to solve difficult math problems. \n \n Aside from my math and programming interests, in my free time I enjoy playing a variety of tabletop games, such as Settlers of Catan (with the Cities and Knights expansion, of course), chess, and D&D. I also love playing basketball and tennis, and occasionally some video games.
